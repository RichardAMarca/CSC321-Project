What Are Edible Game?

Edible game are any animal that can be consumed by humans, by hunting. They can be including deer, elk, pheasant, duck, and turkey. These animals can be found in hunting areas during their designated hunting season.

      Popular Edible Game

      • Mallard: Also known as the wild duck is a dabbling duck that that is commonly found all over the world.
      • Chukar: A partridge commonly found in desert, such as the Mojave Desert. It's also the national bird of Pakistan.
      • California Mule Deer: A game deer that is found in the San Bernardino National Forest.
      • Wild Turkey: A recognizable bird that is found in the San Bernardino National Forest.
      • Black Bear: Not the best tasting animal, it is more for the experience and population control.

      California Department of Fish and Wildlife Regulations

      Click Here For California Department of Fish and Wildlife Regulations

      These are some basic rules and laws that must me followed before you can hunt game.

      • Obtain a valid California Hunting License.
      • Make sure to check the bag limit for every species you wish to collect and get tags of those species, if applicable.
      • For more hunting opportunities, it is recommended to use archery for deer hunting.
      • Be mindful of where you are; avoid disturbing wildlife or damaging plants.
      • Check local regulations to ensure hunting is allowed in your area, and when you are allowed to hunt.
