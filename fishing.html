      What Are Edible Fish?

      Edible fish are any fish that can be consumed by humans, either raw or cooked. They come from various categories, including fish, mollusks, and crustaceans. These fish can be found in either freshwater or saltwater areas.

      Popular Edible Fish

      • Blue Mussels: A common mussel that is found throught the Pacific Ocean.
      • Pacific Razor Clam: A delicious clam that is found under the sands of beaches.
      • Crappie: Despite its unappetizing name, it is considered on of the best tasting freshwater fish.
      • Rainbow Trout: A delicious freshwater fish that is found in colder areas, such as Big Bear or Lake Arrowhead.
      • California Halibut: A large saltwater fish that has a mild, sweet flavor, firm texture, and large white flakes. It's a lean, white fish that's versatile and easy to prepare.

      • Obtain a valid California Fishing License.
      • Make sure to check the bag limit for every species you wish to collect.
      • Be mindful of where you are; avoid disturbing wildlife or damaging plants.
      • Check local regulations to ensure fishing is allowed in your area.
